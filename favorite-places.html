My favorite places

Here are a few places that enchant me:

Dubai

Located in the heart of a desert but you will marvel at the level of development

Silicon Valley

Silicon Valley is a nickname for the southern portion of the San Francisco Bay Area.
It is home for the best technological hubs in the world, Google will not go unmentioned

Paris

a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture.
Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

Machu Picchu

It is located in the Cusco Region, Urubamba Province, Machupicchu District in Peru,
above the Sacred Valley, which is 80 kilometres (50 mi) northwest of Cuzco and through which the Urubamba River flows.
